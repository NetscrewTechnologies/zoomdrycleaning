About Us

This is what we are.

Zoom Dry Cleaning of Pittsburgh – Quality, Full Service, Same Day Service You are smart, confident and you know what you want. Zoom Dry Cleaning of Pittsburgh is a full service dry cleaner that offers you an alternative to the traditional. We’ve combined quality with convenience and made it affordable with value pricing.

We are proud to say that we are:

Locally owned and operated

Fast – offering same day service

Full Service – Alterations, Leather, Silk, Wedding Gowns, Coats, Comforters, etc.

Zoom Dry Cleaning of Pittsburgh is conveniently located at 4638 Browns Hill Rd in the historic Squirrel Hill neighborhood and also in the South Park Shops in Bethel Park. Stop in today to learn about our services or call us at 412-521-2407 (Squirrel Hill) or 412-833-4826 (Bethel Park) .
